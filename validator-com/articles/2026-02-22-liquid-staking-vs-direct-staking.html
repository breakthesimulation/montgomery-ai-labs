Liquid Staking vs Direct Staking on Solana

2026-02-22
Liquid staking lets you use staked SOL as collateral while earning rewards. Marinade Finance and Jito offer liquid staking tokens (LSTs) that can be used in DeFi.Direct staking requires 32 SOL minimum and locks your tokens. While simpler, you lose liquidity. Compare yields accounting for LST DeFi opportunities.
